Traffic cone sneakers

Ankle derby made with veg-tan leather and cone material.

Created for Shoemaking at Rhode Island School of Design, taught by Anne Marika Verploegh Chass√©.

Premise

Strip a repurposed material from its current associations and preconceptions. Create a shoe that is recognizable as its own singular style.

As with most of my designs, I wanted something that catches the eye, can be worn at most occasions, and tells a story. This story begins with a construction cone.

Working on the aesthetic concept of the shoe. In the foreground: tigers I sketched, based on tracing internet cats falling spectacularly from different surfaces.

I wanted to create a world of flashy red and oranges to contrast with the creamy crafts of the veg-tan leather. In this world, roaring tigers plunge into the molten plastic of the sneaker's soles.
